About Anveshanā

A bi-yearly e-magazine resource to celebrate the ideas in and out of the atmosphere.

Our perspective on the world is precisely shaped by the disciplines we engage in daily life, both for economic and mental survival. Some of these disciplines are often in the form of a quest, which is what Anveshanā tries to appreciate. Anveshanā (originally from Sanskrit) means 'quest for knowledge or the hunt'. We aim to unweave these quests into a story of sciences, mathematics, arts, and human thoughts.

Our mission at Anveshanā is to bridge the gap between academic scholarship and artistic curiosity, offering an accessible yet profound confluence of insights. We aim to appreciate every cultural opinion and celebrate the inquiry of lesser-known, overlooked, and hidden inspirations. Our conscience is to imagine a dynamic unity of sciences, mathematics, arts, and human experiences.

In this manner, Anveshanā does not become just a collection of articles on technical subjects but offers to provide a fresh experience of our broad spectrum. Through the tapestry of interviews, articles, and perspectives, Anveshanā offers a human narrative of discoveries and explorations.
